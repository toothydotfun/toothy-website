Donations

Echoes is intended to remain low-maintenance and sustainable to ensure its continuity. We couldn't go on without our Patrons. For considering showing us your support, we thank you kindly. All Patrons pledging amounts greater than $2/mo receive the same perks (we only ask for $2 because of fees associated with Patreon and its payout method, PayPal). Please note that we don't accept donations from children, and we don't take donations in exchange for in-game perks like creative mode, base coordinates, rare items, etc.

Amounts over $2 come with the following perks;

  • Access to an additional 95 !home slots (i.e. the maximum number of !homes you can set increases from 5 to 100).
  • Access to all donor commands, like !pushable, !color, !trail, etc. A full list of donor commands is available on the commands page.
  • Cooldowns for commands like !hub, !random and !skyblock are removed for donors.
  • Patrons have the ability to turn any image of their choosing into an in-game custom map.
  • Patrons may set a custom greeting message from _saltshaker.
  • Patrons may ask to download two region files from the Echoes map for posterity. Coordinates to the region file must be provided. To convert your in-game coordinates from the F3 menu into a region file name, click here to use
  • Dinnerbone's coordinates tool.
